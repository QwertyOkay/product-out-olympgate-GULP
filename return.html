Return Policy: Sculpting Your Satisfaction

At OlympGate, we want you to be thrilled with your garden sculpture. If for any reason you're not satisfied with your purchase, we're here to assist you. Please review our return policy for a smooth return process:

Return Eligibility:

We accept returns under the following conditions:
The return request is initiated within 15 days of receiving your order.
The sculpture is in its original, undamaged condition, including all parts, accessories, and packaging.

How to Initiate a Return:

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:
Contact our customer support team at returns@olympgate.com to request a return authorization (RA) number.
Provide your order number and a brief description of the reason for the return.
Once you receive your RA number, securely package the sculpture with all its components and original packaging.

Return Shipping:

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a product defect or error on our part. We recommend using a trackable and insured shipping method to ensure safe delivery of the return.

You may not use the Website for any unlawful or prohibited purpose.

Inspection and Processing:

Upon receiving your return, our team will inspect the sculpture to ensure it meets our return eligibility criteria. Once approved, we will process your return, and you will receive a refund or exchange based on your preference.

Refunds and Processing Time:

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase. Please allow 7 business days for the refund to be processed after we receive your returned sculpture.

Damaged or Defective Sculptures:

If you receive a damaged or defective sculpture, please contact us at returns@olympgate.com within 10 days of receiving the order. We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy or need assistance with a return, please contact our dedicated customer support team at returns@olympgate.com. We are here to help and ensure your satisfaction.

Our Commitment:

At OlympGate, we are committed to your satisfaction. Our goal is to ensure that you are delighted with your garden sculpture, and our return policy is designed to make the return process as smooth as possible.

Thank you for choosing OlympGate. We appreciate your trust in our sculptures, and we look forward to assisting you with any returns or inquiries you may have.
