Warranty Information: Sculpting Quality and Confidence

At OlympGate, we stand by the craftsmanship and quality of our garden sculptures. We want you to enjoy your outdoor oasis with complete peace of mind. Our warranty reflects our commitment to ensuring your satisfaction with our products.

Limited Warranty:

We proudly offer a limited warranty on our garden sculptures to protect your investment. Our warranty covers the following:

Quality Assurance: We guarantee that our sculptures are crafted using high-quality materials and workmanship to meet or exceed industry standards.

Durability: Our sculptures are built to withstand outdoor conditions. We ensure that they are resistant to weather-related damage such as fading, cracking, or peeling under normal use.

Warranty Duration:

Our limited warranty duration varies by sculpture. The specific warranty period for each product is listed on the respective product page on our website. Please refer to the product description for warranty details.

Warranty Exclusions:

While we strive to provide a comprehensive warranty, it's essential to note that our warranty does not cover the following:

Normal Wear and Tear: Over time, all outdoor items may experience minor wear and tear. This is not covered by our warranty.

Misuse or Neglect: Damage resulting from misuse, neglect, vandalism, or improper maintenance is not covered.

Unauthorized Modifications: Any alterations or unauthorized modifications to the sculpture will void the warranty.

Making a Warranty Claim:

If you believe your sculpture is eligible for a warranty claim, please follow these steps:

Contact our customer support team at warranty@olympgate.com to initiate the warranty process.

Provide proof of purchase, including your order number and purchase date.

Describe the issue you're experiencing with the sculpture and include clear photographs to help us assess the problem.

Resolution:

Upon receiving your warranty claim, our team will review the information provided and determine if the claim is valid. If approved, we will work with you to provide a suitable resolution, which may include repair, replacement, or a refund.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our warranty policy or need assistance with a warranty claim, please contact our dedicated customer support team at warranty@olympgate.com. We are here to assist you promptly.

Our goal is to ensure that your experience with OlympGate and our garden sculptures is as enjoyable and worry-free as possible. Thank you for choosing us as your trusted partner in sculpting your outdoor paradise.
