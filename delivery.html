Shipping Information: Safely Delivering Art to Your Garden

At OlympGate, we understand that your garden sculpture is not just a piece of art; it's a reflection of your taste and a source of inspiration. We're committed to ensuring that your sculpture arrives safely and promptly. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process:

Shipping Methods:

We offer a range of shipping methods to suit your needs:

Standard Shipping: Our standard shipping option ensures safe delivery within 14-20 business days. This option is ideal for customers who want reliable and cost-effective shipping.

Express Shipping: Need your sculpture sooner? Choose our express shipping option to receive your order within 5-10 business days.

Shipping Costs:

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and the selected shipping method. You can view the shipping cost during the checkout process before finalizing your order.

Order Processing:

We strive to process and ship your order as quickly as possible. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

International Shipping:

Yes, we deliver worldwide! We're delighted to bring our garden sculptures to art enthusiasts around the globe. Please note that international shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on your location.

Delivery Timeframes:

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose. Please refer to the estimated delivery time provided during the checkout process.

Tracking Your Order:

We provide tracking information for all shipped orders. You can track the progress of your delivery through the carrier's website using the provided tracking number.

Delivery Inspection:

Upon receiving your sculpture, we recommend inspecting the packaging for any visible damage. If the packaging appears damaged, please make a note of it when signing for the delivery.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding shipping, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team at shipping@olympgate.com. We're here to assist you with any inquiries or assistance you may need.

Shipping Updates:

We will keep you informed about the status of your order via email. You will receive notifications when your order is processed, shipped, and out for delivery.

At OlympGate, we take pride in delivering garden sculptures that bring beauty and inspiration to your outdoor space. Our commitment to a safe and efficient shipping process ensures that your sculpture arrives in pristine condition.

Thank you for choosing OlympGate. We look forward to enhancing your garden with our exquisite sculptures.
